About Safar Ghar

Safar Ghar is your gateway to authentic experiences in Pakistan's stunning Northern Areas. We connect travelers with local hosts in Hunza, Skardu, Gilgit, and other breathtaking locations.

Unlike commercial hotels, our homestays allow you to live with local families, eat home-cooked meals, and experience the region's traditions firsthand. Your stay directly benefits the community and helps preserve their way of life.

From the majestic peaks of K2 to the serene valleys of Swat, we offer comfortable accommodations that put you at the heart of Pakistan's most spectacular landscapes.
